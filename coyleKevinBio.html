ABOUT ME

“Everyone you will ever meet knows something you don't.” - Bill Nye

Every bit of knowledge no matter how big or small sheds a little more light on the darkness of the unknown. In my work I strive to find ways of organizing, analyzing, and visualizing data that provide individuals with the clearest view of the answers that are often obscured by the mess often found in real life data. The interdisciplinary skill set and approach to problem solving that has served me well throughout my career and education provides a unique vantage point for handling complex data and multi-faceted problems. The proactive, preventative approach I first witnessed in my clinical experiences in medicine inspires me to use my technical abilities in data science and epidemiology to assit in identifying key drivers that impact the etiology and pathogenesis of disease.

Interdisciplinary research is my passion. I love to work with people from diverse cultural and educational backgrounds that bring unique perspectives to projects. I value confidence, transparency, integrity, and kindness as I believe these are the attributes that enable people to come together and build great solutions. My backgroud is diverse, with professional experinces in clinical trial research, machine learning, and clinical medicine. I have extensive experince working as statisticain counsulting on several clinical trial grade research projects and building/tuning ML models.
